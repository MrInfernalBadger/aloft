Grace completed her Diploma of Remedial Massage Therapy at Evolve College and founded Aloft in early 2023. She is trained in both remedial and relaxation massage and can offer personalised strength and conditioning programs for injury prevention. Grace is passionate about creating a relaxed environment for people new to receiving massage. Outside work, she enjoys distance running, ultimate frisbee and vegetarian cooking.

Remedial Massage

A therapeutic treatment aimed at addressing muscle pain, tension, and injuries. It involves deep tissue techniques to promote healing and improve overall physical well-being.

Relaxation Massage

A gentle treatment designed to reduce stress and promote relaxation. It uses smooth, gliding strokes to improve circulation and ease muscle tension.
