Grace completed her Diploma of Remedial Massage Therapy at Evolve College and founded Aloft in early 2023. She is trained in both remedial and relaxation massage and can offer personalised strength and conditioning programs for injury prevention. Grace is passionate about creating a welcoming environment, especially for people new to receiving massage and is always happy to tailor treatments for neurodiverse clients - everyone deserves the benefits of massage! Outside work, she enjoys distance running, ultimate frisbee and cryptic crosswords.

Remedial Massage

Remedial massage is a bespoke treatment with an outcome in mind based on specific complaints. It can be used to address muscle and joint pain, provide pain relief and promote healing from injury. It can involve elements of relaxation massage, as well as deep tissue techniques, trigger point therapy, joint jostling and stretching.

Relaxation Massage

Relaxation massage is a gentle treatment, designed to reduce stress and promote calm in the body and mind. Smooth, warming strokes are used to improve circulation and ease muscle tension. Relaxation massage is suitable for people of all ages and frequent treatment can counter the negative effects of stress on the body.
